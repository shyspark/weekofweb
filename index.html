Science and Nature

All the silence that comes is not peace, All endings do not bring something new.
Life and death are natures simple circles,
And what is perception is not always true.

There are frequencies of nth dimensions,
And miles of depth beyond this point, this place.
The instruments used to collect the data,
Cannot account outside their own time and space.

It requires terminology not yet defined, Where gravity and color do not exist. You have to let go of all preconceived notions,
Before you can understand what you have missed.

Purpose only matters here and now, Because people believe in something more.
All beginnings do not follow somethings end,
And all the noise that comes is not war.
